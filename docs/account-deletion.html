Account & Data Deletion

PingU - Anonymous Chat App

Request Account and Data Deletion
Follow the steps below to permanently delete your PingU account and ALL associated personal data. Deleting your account will remove all your personal information, messages, chats, and preferences.

How to Delete Your Account and Data

Option 1: Delete from App (Recommended)

The easiest way to delete your account is directly from the PingU app:

  1. Open the PingU app on your device
  2. Tap the menu icon (three dots) in the top right corner
  3. Select "Delete Account" from the menu
  4. Read the warning message carefully
  5. Tap "Delete Forever" to confirm
  6. Your account will be permanently deleted immediately
Instant Deletion: Account deletion from the app is completed immediately. You will be automatically signed out and all your data will be permanently removed.

Option 2: Email Request (Alternative)

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion via email:

  1. Subject Line: "Account Deletion Request - [Your Email]"
  2. Send to: gsatnitwmechias@gmail.com
  3. Include:
    • Your PingU account email address
    • Confirmation: "I want to permanently delete my PingU account"
    • Reason for deletion (optional)

Email Support (for issues or questions):

Email: gsatnitwmechias@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Response Time: Within 7 business days

Note: In-app deletion is instant. Email is only for cases where you cannot access the app.

What Personal Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following personal data will be permanently and irreversibly deleted:

  • Account Information: Email address, user ID, registration date
  • Profile Data: Age, gender, preferences, bio information
  • Chat Data: All your messages and conversation history with other users
  • Match Data: Your match preferences and search history
  • Social Data: Your blocked users list and reported users
  • Reports Data: Your moderation reports and appeal submissions
  • Authentication: Password and all authentication credentials

Important Note

Complete Data Deletion: PingU does NOT offer partial data deletion. Deleting your account is the only way to remove your personal data. You cannot selectively delete specific pieces of information without deleting your entire account.

What Does NOT Get Deleted

The following data will be retained for legal compliance:

  • Analytics Data: Anonymized aggregate statistics and usage patterns (no personal identifiers)
  • Legal Records: Compliance records if required by law or regulation
  • Backup Retention: Backup data may be retained for up to 30 days for system recovery purposes
Important Warning:
  • Account deletion is PERMANENT and cannot be undone
  • You will lose access to all your chats and conversations
  • You will need to create a new account if you want to use PingU again
  • This action cannot be reversed

Processing Timeline

In-App Deletion (Option 1):

  • Immediate (Day 0): Your account and personal data are permanently deleted
  • Automatic sign-out: You'll be logged out instantly
  • Days 0-30: Backup data retention period (automated system backups only)
  • Day 30+: All backups removed, data permanently erased

Email Deletion (Option 2):

  • Day 0: You submit your deletion request via email
  • Days 0-7: We verify your identity and process the request
  • Day 7: Your account and personal data are permanently deleted
  • Days 7-37: Backup data retention period
  • Day 37+: All backups removed, data permanently erased
Data Retention Summary:
• In-app deletion: Data deleted immediately, backups removed after 30 days
• Email deletion: Data deleted within 7 days, backups removed after 30 days
• Total retention period: Maximum 37 days from deletion request

Verification Process

For In-App Deletion:

  • You must be logged into your account
  • Confirmation dialog is required
  • No additional verification needed

For Email Deletion:

  • We verify that the email address matches your account
  • We may confirm your request via email for security
  • Processing takes up to 7 business days
