Follow the steps below to permanently delete your PingU account and all associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete from App (Recommended)

The easiest way to delete your account is directly from the PingU app:

  1. Open the PingU app on your device
  2. Tap the menu icon (three dots) in the top right corner
  3. Select "Delete Account" from the menu
  4. Read the warning message carefully
  5. Tap "Delete Forever" to confirm
  6. Your account will be permanently deleted immediately
Instant Deletion: Account deletion from the app is completed immediately. You will be automatically signed out and all your data will be permanently removed.

Option 2: Email Request (Alternative)

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion via email:

  1. Subject Line: "Account Deletion Request - [Your Email]"
  2. Send to: gsatnitwmechias@gmail.com
  3. Include:
    • Your PingU account email address
    • Confirmation: "I want to permanently delete my PingU account"
    • Reason for deletion (optional)

Email Support (for issues or questions):

Email: gsatnitwmechias@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Response Time: Within 7 business days

Note: In-app deletion is instant. Email is only for cases where you cannot access the app.

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently and irreversibly deleted:

  • Your user account and profile information
  • Your chat messages and conversation history
  • Your match preferences and search history
  • Your blocked users list
  • Your reports and moderation records
  • All authentication data and credentials

What Does NOT Get Deleted

The following data will be retained for legal compliance:

  • Anonymized aggregate statistics and analytics data
  • Legal compliance records (if required by law)
  • Backup data may be retained for up to 30 days
Important Warning:
  • Account deletion is PERMANENT and cannot be undone
  • You will lose access to all your chats and conversations
  • You will need to create a new account if you want to use PingU again
  • This action cannot be reversed

Processing Timeline

In-App Deletion (Option 1):

  • Immediate: Your account is permanently deleted right away
  • Automatic sign-out: You'll be logged out instantly
  • Days 1-30: Backup data retention period

Email Deletion (Option 2):

  • Day 1: You submit your deletion request via email
  • Days 1-7: We verify your identity and process the request
  • Day 7: Your account is permanently deleted
  • Days 7-37: Backup data retention period

Verification Process

For In-App Deletion:

  • You must be logged into your account
  • Confirmation dialog is required
  • No additional verification needed

For Email Deletion:

  • We verify that the email address matches your account
  • We may confirm your request via email for security
  • Processing takes up to 7 business days
